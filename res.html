Best Heroes Around!

The Avengers are a team of extraordinary individuals, with either superpowers or other special characteristics. Its purpose was to protect global stability from inner or extraterrestrial threats.

Location

The New Avengers Facility is the primary base of operations used by the Avengers after the defeat of Ultron and the establishment of the team's new roster.

Watch 'em work

The Avengers are without a doubt the most reliable and efficient heroes around, and can you see it for yourself in The Avengers(2012), Avengers: Age of Ultron(2015) among others.

The Avengers Tower

Originally called the Stark Tower, it was renamed the Avengers Tower when all other letters kinda, fell off, during the battle of New York. It then became the first headquarters of the Avengers.

The Hide-out

Clint Barton's safe-house, which turned out to be his actual house, turned out to be quite useful when the Avengers needed a hide-out, since Ultron kinda had eyes everwhere.

The New Avengers Facility

This compound, spread over several acres, was specially designed for the Avengers by Tony Stark. It is an ideal place for all kinds of 'avengers stuff', like training, meetings etc.
